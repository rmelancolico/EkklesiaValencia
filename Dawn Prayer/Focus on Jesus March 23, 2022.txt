3 ways to keep in focus


1. Create routine in daily life that focuses on God
  - Worship and give thanks to God everyday
  - dont let the fire goes down
  - daily devotional, daily dawn prayer,

2. remove distractions and people that keeps you away from Christ
  - dont do the ungodly things
  - choose your companions
  - dili ikaw ang ma kabig, ikaw dapat mangabig

3. surround youself with the reminders of God
  - fill yourself everyday with the presence of God
  - remember the promises
