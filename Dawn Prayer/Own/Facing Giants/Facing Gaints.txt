2. Receive anointing
   
   - before David face Goliath he was first anointed by Samuel
   - sa story ni David, everything start when Samuel anoint David
   - si David shepherd boy lng, pero once na g anoint cya ni Samuel,from shepherd boy nahimo cya into Giant Slayer
   - when facing our giants, importante kaayo na maka receive ta ug anointing.
   - unsa naa sa anointing?
   - anointng is God's confirmation of His choice
      - sa time na g reject na ni Lord si King Saul, g command ni Lord si Samuel na mag adto cya sa balay ni Jesse, 
        David's father
      - ato na time wala pa nag kita silang Samuel ug David, Wala pa kaila si Samuel kang David
      - Pero sa diyang nakita na ni Samuel si David, The Lord said to Samuel in 1 Samuel 16:12 "This is the one -
        anoint him!"
      - this confirm na si David ang na pili ni Lord to become the leader of Isreal.

   - Empowerment for leadership
      - then after sa pag anoint kang David in 1 Samuel 16:13 it says there "Immediately the Spirit of the Lord took
        control of David and was with him from that day on"
      - na recall nako tong g ingon ni pastora last macro life group na, samtang buhi pa ta we will face challenges
      - pero with anointing ma over come nato tanan nato na mga giants sa atong kinabuhi
      - sa akong experience dili lng man financial struggles na giant ang na atubang nako. naa pd mga ginagmay na mga
        giants, pero tungod kay na empower ko as a leader, na conquer
