What is grace

 - Grace is God to bless us rather than to curse us by our sins

Why grace
 - we cannot afford it
 - we only recieve it from God through Jesus Christ

grace will take effect, mo flow in our life the moment naay tinood na lordship si Lord sa atong life
making Lord as lordship is a choice

  - wala na dapat ta sa mindset sa wordliness

2. enabling grace

  - ang Word sa bal.an nimo dya sa imong devo

 
Grace of God
 Dili jd ta arangan sa gpang hatag ni Lord sa atong life. But we receive it freely. We didnt pay for it. Thats how God loves us. With that, we need to response properly sa ghatag na grace sa atoa. We are save to save! The proper way to response to  the grace is that we need to effectively manifest it, transmit it, and deliver it.
Manifest: we need to show it that we are save by grace. live the life of Jesus. be like Jesus. Dili nato e hide kung uns ang na receive nato
Transmit: continues invitation sa mga tao sa palibot para maka receive pud sila sa grace na. we are enabled, empowered to do our duty
deliver: show output. fruitfulness is the  outcome of true faith
