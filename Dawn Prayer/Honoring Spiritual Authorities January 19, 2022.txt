Honoring Spiritual Authorities

Exodus 17:8-13

How do we honor our spiritual authorities

F A S T DISCIPLES

F - Faithul => Aaron and Hur as own initiative, wala na sila nag hulat na mag ingon si Moses na mo ingon, 'need nako ug bangko, gkapoy ko' nag act ra sa ilang nakita sa ilang leader

A - Available => after nila gbutang ang bangko, wala sila nag stop dya. still they may themselve available to Moses by standing beside Moses and help his hands. 

S - Submissive => Joshua submitted to Moses during sa pinakalit na battle

T - Teachable => dawat ready ta mo dawat sa mga teaching sa atong leader
